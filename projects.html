My Projects

Here are a few projects I've worked on (or at least tried to work on):

Project 1: Hackathon SS4

Description: Our very first project focused on developing a digital platform to support mother and child health management. The system is designed to securely store and organize data related to a child’s health journey, beginning at birth and continuing through to complete vaccination and nutritional milestones. By centralizing this information, the project aims to improve accessibility, ensure timely health interventions, and contribute to better long-term outcomes for children and their families. start somewhere!👩🏻‍🍼

Project 2: Khmer Enterprise 2023

Description: We further refined this idea beyond the hackathon stage, developing it into a practical and impactful solution. This journey ultimately led us to Khmer Enterprise 2023, where our team was honored with the Champion award for innovation and execution.🏆

Project 3: Alarm Clock That Judges You

Description: A website that won’t stop ringing until you answer a coding trivia question. Bonus: it roasts you if you hit snooze.🛌

Note: What began as a blinking “Hello World!” (yes, it hurt people’s eyes 👀) somehow grew into a real health platform for mothers and children. We kept pushing it, and against all odds, it actually worked! The project went from “just for fun at a hackathon” to Champion of Khmer Enterprise 2023. Not bad for something that started with copy-paste code. 🚀
